[size=4][b]What It Does[/b][/size]

QLP is a [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/site/mods/1]Vortex[/url]  "Quick Link" plugin/extension for [url=https://phostwood.github.io/crash-analyzer/skyrim.html]Phostwood's Crash Log Analyzer[/url]. From crash to analysis in [b]zero clicks[/b] — QLP now detects new crash logs when Skyrim closes/crashes and can automatically upload them to [b]Sovnkrasch[/b] (our dedicated cloud storage for Skyrim crash logs) and open the analyzer in your browser, preloaded and ready to review within seconds.

Fully configurable: enable Local-Only mode to skip the upload entirely, adjust auto-trigger behavior, set crash log paths and filename patterns, and more — all from Vortex's settings panel. Or trigger it manually anytime from Vortex's upper-right menu.

Uploaded logs get a permanent* shareable link — useful when asking for help, sharing an interesting crash as an example, or contributing to the broader pool of researchable Skyrim crash data.

[list]
[*][b]Auto-Finds Your Log[/b] — Locates your most recent crash log automatically. Supports the preferred CrashLoggerSSE out of the box; paths and filename patterns are configurable.
[*][b]Zero-Click Analysis[/b] — Detects new crash logs when Skyrim closes and can automatically open the analyzer. No need to open Vortex's upper-right menu after a crash. Configurable: prompt each time, analyze silently, or disable entirely.
[*][b]Local-Only Mode[/b] — Skip the upload entirely. The analyzer opens in your browser and automatically copies your crash log into your clipboard, letting you paste your log in manually.
[*][b]Automatic Fallback[/b] — If an upload fails, the plugin falls back to local-only behavior automatically so you're never left without a result.
[*][b]Configurable[/b] — Adjust auto-trigger behavior, crash log folder path, filename patterns, and more from Vortex's settings panel. Defaults cover most setups without any configuration.
[*][b]Shareable Links[/b] — Uploaded logs get a permanent link you can share with others — no expiration*, no fighting Pastebin size limits**.
[*][b]Shareable URL History[/b] — Every analyzer link is automatically saved to a local log file after a successful upload, so you always have a record of your past analyses to share on Reddit, Discord, or NexusMods forums.
[/list]
[i]* "Permanent" in theory, but I'm trying to keep this all under free tier storage, so it's possible the old logs may eventually get aged out[/i]

[i]** For comparison: pastebin.com caps at 512 KB uncompressed. Sovnkrasch accepts up to 2 MB compressed — so even monster logs fit (tested up to 35 MB).[/i]

[size=4][b]Powered by Sovnkrasch[/b][/size]

[img]https://phostwood.github.io/crash-analyzer/images/Sovnkrasch-339x339.png[/img]

When you choose to upload, QLP sends your log to [i]Sovnkrasch[/i] — a cloud storage service built specifically for Skyrim crash logs, running on Cloudflare Workers and R2. The name came from Kyler45: [i]sovn[/i] means "sleep" in Norwegian, [i]krasch[/i] means "crash." A crash log's final resting place.

[b]What makes it different:[/b]

[list]
[*][b]Privacy-first[/b] — No personal information collected; Windows usernames are automatically redacted from file paths. [url=https://phostwood.github.io/crash-analyzer/PrivacyPolicy.html]Full privacy policy.[/url]
[*][b]Skyrim-only[/b] — Validates uploads to ensure only actual Skyrim crash logs are accepted.
[*][b]Smart deduplication[/b] — Upload the same log twice and you get the same link back.
[*][b]Handles big logs[/b] — Accepts up to 2 MB compressed. A 35 MB uncompressed log has been successfully uploaded.**
[/list]
[i]** For comparison: pastebin.com caps at 512 KB uncompressed. Sovnkrasch accepts up to 2 MB compressed — so even monster logs fit (tested up to 35 MB).[/i]

[size=4][b]Installation & Setup[/b]
[/size][b]
New [url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tWRaBMAb7sA]Demo and Installation Video[/url]﻿﻿ ﻿by [url=https://www.youtube.com/@Battdapsycho]Battdapsycho
[/url][/b]
All you need is [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/site/mods/1]Vortex[/url] and a crash log with the default filename format — e.g. [font=Courier New]crash-2026-02-16-04-41-28.log[/font]. [i][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/59818]Crash Logger SSE[/url]﻿ [b]highly recommended[/b]. [/i]Takes about five minutes.

[b]1. Download the Extension[/b]
Grab [font=Courier New]VortexExtension-PhostwoodQLP.zip[/font] from the Files tab (or [url=https://github.com/Phostwood/crash-analyzer/tree/main/VortexExtension-PhostwoodQLP]GitHub[/url]).

[b]2. Install It in Vortex[/b]
In Vortex, go to [font=Courier New]Extensions[/font] and either drag and drop the zip onto the [font=Courier New]Drop File(s)[/font] area in the bottom right, or click it to browse for the file.

[b]3. Restart Vortex[/b]
The extension loads automatically. Click the [font=Courier New]Restart[/font] button when prompted, or close and reopen Vortex manually.

[b]4. Run It[/b]
You can also manually run it by selecting [font=Courier New]🐛 QLP: Analyze Crash Log[/font] in Vortex's upper-right menu, or by clicking the .[font=Courier New]🐛 Analyze Crash Log[/font] button in the Vortex Dashboard.

[b]5. Optional: Configure It[/b]
Adjust your crash log folder path and filename pattern at [font=Courier New]Settings → Phostwood's QLP[/font].

Default configurations (clear any field and restart Vortex to reset it to its default value):
Enable plugin: [code]true[/code]
Local Only Mode: [code]false[/code]
Auto-trigger behavior: [code]prompt[/code]
Crash Analyzer URL: [code]https://phostwood.github.io/crash-analyzer/skyrim.html[/code]
Cloudflare Worker URL: [code]https://skyrim-crashlog-worker.phostwood.workers.dev/upload[/code]
Crash Log Directory: (auto-detected from your My Documents folder on first run)
Crash Log Filename Patterns: [code]crash-*.log, Crash_*.txt[/code]
Shareable URL Log: (auto-detected on first run; stores a history of all your analyzer links)
Last Processed Log Time: (auto-managed; clear to force re-trigger on next detection)

These settings are accessible in Vortex at [font=Courier New]Settings → Phostwood's QLP[/font].
