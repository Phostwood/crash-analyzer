# Phostwood's Crash Analyzers:

With over 450 hours of development, this free, open source, advanced analyzer examines Nolvus and general Skyrim crash logs to help diagnose and fix 80-90% of crashes, providing well-researched troubleshooting steps and links. It's currently helping about 100 different Skyrim modders per day, and stands apart from other analyzers with its advanced diagnoses and frequent updates.
